Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

SOURFACEMUSIC uses this website to share music-production services, education, official social links, booking information and the official fundraiser gateway. This policy describes the basic information that may be handled when people visit or contact the site.

Information you send

The booking form may include your artist name, email address, project details and requested service. FormSubmit delivers the inquiry to SOURFACEMUSIC by email.

Website data

GitHub Pages, browsers, advertising services and security providers may process technical data such as browser type, device type, requested pages, approximate location, referral source and visit time.

Advertising and cookies

The site may use Google AdSense. Advertising providers may use cookies or similar technology to measure performance, prevent fraud and comply with advertising rules.

Fundraiser and linked platforms

Donations are completed on GoFundMe. Social networks, GoFundMe, FormSubmit and other linked services apply their own privacy policies. This site does not collect or store payment-card details.

For privacy questions, email sourfacemusic@gmail.com. This policy may change as the website adds or removes services.
