FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a beat production take?

Turnaround is typically 3-7 days depending on complexity and revisions. Rush orders available.

Do you offer leasing or exclusive rights?

Both! Leasing rights for immediate use, exclusive licensing available for artists wanting sole ownership.

What's your remix process?

We take your original track and create clean, performance-ready blends optimized for DJ sets and performances.

Can you help with artist development?

Yes. We offer guidance on song selection, rollout strategy, branding, and catalog organization.

Do you offer consulting services?

Absolutely. Book a session to discuss your project, goals, and get custom recommendations.

How do I purchase merch or beats?

Visit our Gear Shop for official merch, or access beats directly through SoundCloud, YouTube, and Facebook.
